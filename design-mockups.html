Final Design Selection

âœ… Selected: Clean & Readable with Compressed Noble Tokens

A cohesive, minimalist design system with consistent layout across both cards and nobles.

Regular Cards (140Ã—200px)

  • Colored band at top (20% height)
  • Points top-right in band
  • Costs stacked bottom-left
  • Level bottom-right (small)
  • Dark background, minimal styling

Noble Tokens (130Ã—85px - Squat)

  • NO colored band
  • Points top-right (larger relative)
  • Costs stacked bottom-left (larger relative)
  • NO level indicator
  • Same dark background
Design Philosophy: Clean, focused on gameplay. Colors clearly indicate gem costs/bonuses. Consistent layout pattern makes information quick to scan. Noble tokens are visually distinct from regular cards by their smaller size but maintain the same organizational principle.
